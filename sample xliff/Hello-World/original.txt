<h2>A Nested List</h2>
<p>List can be nested (lists <b>inside</b> lists):</p>

<ul>
    <li>Coffee</li>
    <li>
        Many <i>varieties</i> of Tea
        <ul>
            <li>Black tea</li>
            <li>Green tea</li>
        </ul>
        Many <i>varieties</i> of Tea
    </li>
    <li>Milk</li>
</ul>

<p>That was it!</p>
